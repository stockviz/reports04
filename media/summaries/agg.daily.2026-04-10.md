### **Expert Investment Advice (Mohit Gang & Shweta Rajani)**
| Strategy | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| **SIPs** | Long-term benefit despite short-term dips |
| **Lump-sum** | Best for bonuses/windfalls |
| **STP (Systematic Transfer Plan)** | Ideal for capital preservation + growth |
| **Three-Bucket Approach** | 1. Regular SIPs (salary) 2. Lump-sum/STP (windfalls) 3. Valuation-based timing |
| **Step-up SIPs** | Great for inflation-proofing |
| **Debt SIPs** | Best for short-term goals |
| **SWP (Systematic Withdrawal Plan)** | **Superior to dividends** for retirees (tax-efficient) |
| **Budgeting Rule** | **50-30-20**: 20% of income to investments (mix of equity & debt) |
