### 🏢 **Corporate & Financial Highlights**

### 🏗️ **Infrastructure & Real Estate**

- **Mumbai Redevelopment Boom**: 
  - **Speculative frenzy** replacing need-based redevelopment.  
  - Societies demanding **150% extra area**, high corpus funds, inflated rents — often driven by aggressive developers and unqualified PMCs.  
  - **Risk of crisis**: Market downturn could stall projects, leaving residents **homeless and financially stranded**.  
  - **Call to action**: Prioritize **proven, financially sound developers** and responsible PMCs. RERA responsibilities emphasized.

- **APCO Infratech**: Exploring sale of **Z-Morh 6.5 km tunnel (J&K)** to **Alpha Alternatives** for **$267M EV**.  
  - Part of 15-year DBFOT concession with NHIDCL; generates ₹295 cr semi-annual annuity.  
  - Reflects **rising infrastructure monetization trend** — govt has already monetized ₹1.52L cr in assets, with 13,400 km PPP pipeline for FY26–FY28.

  - Altman apologized, met officials, pledged to improve prevention — **sparking debate on tech’s legal duty to report violence**.
