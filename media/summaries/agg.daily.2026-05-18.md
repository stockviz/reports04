### 💼 **Corporate Strategy & Restructuring**
#### **Gautam Adani Group**:
- Overhauling conglomerate via **outsourcing** (manufacturing, logistics, engineering) to third-party vendors.
- Flattening hierarchy to **3 reporting layers** for faster decisions.
- **Hiring freeze** on external senior hires; internal promotions via portal (exceptions by Karan Adani).
- Aims: **Capital efficiency**, support for **₹2+ trillion capex**, and operational agility — *mirroring Apple’s contract model*.
- **Risks**: Governance, cyber, ESG, and talent diversity in decentralized vendor ecosystem.

#### **Meta (Facebook)**:
- **8,000 layoffs (10% workforce)** on May 20, following 2022–23 cuts of 21,000.
- Severe anxiety among staff: stockpiling snacks, drinks, chargers.
- **Severance**: 16 weeks’ salary + 2 weeks/year of service + 18 months health benefits ("golden parachute").
- **Rationale**: Streamlining teams, AI-driven productivity, fewer management layers.
- New hires report **poor response rates** after listing Meta on résumés.

#### **Uber**:
- Reinvesting profits from mature markets (U.S./Europe) into **high-growth India** despite short-term losses.
- Expanding **two-wheeler services**, trialing **subscription models**, scaling **EV fleets**.
- Facing regulatory pushback and competition (e.g., Rapido).
- Ambitions: **Autonomous taxis**, **super-app ecosystem**, long-term profitability.
- Focus: **Innovation over moats**, flexible culture, personal commitment.

#### **Samsung**:
- **45,000 workers** to strike for **18 days (May 21)** over **bonus disparity**:
  - Memory chip staff: up to **607% bonus**.
  - Logic/Foundry (AI chips): only **50–100%**.
- Unions demand **removal of 50% bonus cap** and **15% share of operating profit** for all.
- **Potential $14–21B impact** on profitability; global supply chain risks.
- Government and investors alarmed.
