### ⚠️ **Critical Industrial Disruptions Due to LPG/Natural Gas Shortages**
- **Borosil** halts **one of three glass furnaces** in Jaipur due to **insufficient LPG/natural gas supply**.
  - Furnaces **cannot switch to induction tech** (only applicable for metal melting).
  - Company is in talks with oil firms and regulators; expects **partial restart soon**, but **no timeline**.
  - Daily shutdowns incur **significant costs** (exact figures undisclosed).
- **Other affected companies**:
  - **Mahanagar Gas Ltd (MGL)**
  - **GAIL**
  - **Gujarat Narmada Fertilizers & Chemicals (GNFC)**
  - **Hindustan Organic Chemicals**
  - **Royal Orchids, Karmathotla, IDPA, EMS players**
- **Government prioritizes household LPG** over commercial use → **commercial gas allocations cut**.

### 💡 **Policy & Substitution Options**
- **Induction cookers** promoted as **LPG substitute for homes/offices**, but **not viable for glass/metal furnaces**.
- **Government is proactively managing LPG stocks**, but **no immediate relief** for industrial users.

> 🔴 **Risk**: Prolonged shutdowns → **cost inflation**, **supply chain delays**, **margin pressure** across chemicals, glass, fertilizers, and consumer durables.
