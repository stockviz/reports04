## ⚖️ **POLICY & REGULATORY**

### **India’s DPDP Act (Digital Personal Data Protection)**
- **Deadline**: **13 May 2027**.
- **Startup Challenge**:
  - No size-based exemption — all entities must comply.
  - Key obligations:
    - Identify and map personal data.
    - Obtain **free, specific, informed consent**.
    - Update data processing agreements (processors must delete data post-use).
    - **AI-driven firms**: Must **mask/filter personal data**, disclose AI processing in privacy notices.
- **Compliance Burden**: 20% increase in workload; time- and resource-intensive.
- **Advice**: Treat startups as **data fiduciaries** — even with limited resources.
