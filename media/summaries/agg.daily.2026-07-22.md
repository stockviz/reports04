#### 💊 **Trump’s Generic Drug Tariff Plan: Market Shock & Strategic Reassessment**
- **Policy Announced:**  
  - **0% tariff** on generic drug imports for **2 years (starting Aug 1)**  
  - **100% tariff** in **Year 3**  
  - **200% tariff** thereafter  
- **Impact on Indian Pharma:**  
  - India supplies **~40% of US generics** ($10B+ value), with **670 FDA-registered plants abroad**.
  - **High US exposure firms at risk:**  
    - **Granules India (76%)**  
    - **Lupin (42%)**  
    - **Gland Pharma (53%)**  
    - **Zydus (40%)**  
  - **Low-exposure firms (Alkem, Torrent)** likely unaffected directly — but **market sentiment** may dip.
- **Resilient Players:**  
  - **Aurobindo, Senor, Dr. Reddy’s, Biocon** — already building US manufacturing capacity.
- **Market Reaction:** Immediate anxiety, especially in pharma stocks; **uncertainty remains high** due to timing (aligned with end of Trump’s term).
- **Analysts’ View:** Policy may be **more symbolic than implementable**, but **supply chain adjustments are inevitable**.
