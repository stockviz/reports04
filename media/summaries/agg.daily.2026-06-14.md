  - **Opportunities**: For Indian firms in construction, power, cooling, networking, and equipment.
  - **Environmental concerns**: High water/power use; need for **renewables, water reuse, and guardrails**.
  - Goal: Capture a share of the **$300B AI-driven data centre market** while balancing **sustainability and inclusive growth**.
