**Market Outlook: Weak Opening Expected Amid Global Cues, Oil Dip, and Mixed Earnings**

The Indian markets are poised for a **bearish opening**, with the **GIFT Nifty showing a 110-point gap down** following a trading holiday. Despite positive sentiment from two consecutive days of overseas sessions and record highs in U.S. equities — driven by AI momentum and optimism around a potential 60-day U.S.-Iran cease-fire extension — **oil prices remain at multi-year lows**, pressuring energy-sensitive sectors and broader market sentiment.

**Key Global Cues:**
- **U.S. Markets**: S&P 500 and Nasdaq closed at record highs (+0.6% and +1% respectively), fueled by AI-driven stocks (Dell, Nvidia, Microsoft) and strong earnings (e.g., Dell, Meta). AI news was dominant: **Anthropic raised $6.5B in Series H**, pushing its valuation to **$965B** — surpassing OpenAI — with massive hyperscaler commitments (Amazon: $5B, total potential $25B). Meta also raised 2026 AI capex to $125–145B and previewed paid AI subscriptions.
- **Asia**: Taiwan, Korea, and Japan rose ~2%, while Shanghai remained flat. **Samsung shipped 12-layer HBM chips**, and **France’s Mistral AI eyeing custom silicon**.
- **Geopolitics**: Oman affirmed it has no plans to toll the Strait of Hormuz; Trump reiterated demands for freedom of navigation. However, **no official U.S.-Iran ceasefire announcement**, keeping markets cautious.

**Domestic Focus:**
- **Earnings Watch**: Mixed results:
  - **Ashok Leyland** beat estimates.
  - **Bharat Dynamics** and **Deepak Fertilizer** posted sharp profit declines.
  - **PJ Electroplast** missed revenue and margin guidance.
  - **Finolex Cables** beat expectations.
  - **Physio-Viva** narrowed losses.
- **Key Stocks to Watch**:
  - **Reliance**: Annual report out — market awaiting strategic updates.
  - **PB Fintech**: Block deal activity raises speculation.
  - **MCX**: Potential UBS downgrade risk.
- **F&O Data**: Increased short interest, active strikes between **23,600–24,500**, suggesting **range-bound trading**. Nifty must hold **23,800** (or 23,719 for weekly close) and aim for **24,000–24,500 resistance**. **Nifty Bank** needs to stay above **55,000**.

**Market Structure & Sentiment:**
- **FI activity**: Net selling of futures, adding short contracts — bearish bias.
- **VIX fell below 15**, indicating reduced volatility.

**Other Developments:**
- **Pfizer & Innovent**: Signed **$10.5B global oncology deal** — $650M upfront, co-developing 12 cancer programs.
- **Anthropic’s $6.5B raise**: Total capital now over **$130B**, run-rate revenue hits **$47B**. Plans for near-term IPO, scaling Claude Opus 4.8.

### **Summary Snapshot:**
- **Market Outlook**: **Bearish open** (GIFT Nifty gap down), **range-bound trading expected**.
- **Key Drivers**: Oil dip, mixed earnings, F&O positioning, geopolitical uncertainty, AI hype.
- **Critical Levels**: Nifty support at **23,800**, resistance at **24,000–24,500**; Nifty Bank support at **55,000**.
- **AI Momentum**: Global AI boom continues — Anthropic’s $965B valuation, Meta’s AI push, chip supply chain advances.
