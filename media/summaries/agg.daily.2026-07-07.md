### 🚀 **Space & Tech: Skyroot Aerospace’s Leap to Commercial Launch**
**Skyroot Aerospace**, founded by ex-ISRO engineers **Pawan Kumar Chandana** and **Naga Bharath Daka**, is on the cusp of becoming **India’s first private company to launch a commercial satellite** with the **Vikram-1** orbital rocket (capable of delivering ~300 kg to LEO).

🔹 **Key Highlights**:
- **Sub-orbital success achieved in 2022**
- **₹100 crore in pre-contract revenue** generated
- **Domestic supply chain**: ~400 Indian suppliers; talent from ISRO and returning expats
- **Roadmap**: Vikram-1 (200–300 kg), Vikram-2 (1 ton), and future reusable rockets
- **Goal**: Achieve **one launch per month** within five years
- **Market Opportunity**: Global launch demand outstrips supply; India’s space policy (IN-SPACe) enabling private sector entry

📌 **Next Phase**: Seeking **anchor government contracts** to accelerate growth and scale.
