## 🌍 **Geopolitical & Macro: U.S.–Iran Tensions & Oil Market Dynamics**

### 🔻 **Oil Price Volatility & Strategic Implications**
- **Brent crude slipped below $100** after ~80 days, driven by **falling tensions** in U.S.–Iran talks and hopes of Strait of Hormuz reopening.
- However, **market deadlock persists**: Trump’s mixed signals, stalled nuclear negotiations, and continued closure of the Strait have led to **severe global oil inventory drawdowns**.
- **Vandana Hari warns**: The market underestimates the **"higher-for-longer"** price outlook. Even with a deal, **reopening will be gradual and patchy**, leading to **only modest declines**, not a collapse.
- **Implication**: Oil remains a **high-risk, high-volatility asset**; supply disruptions are now priced in, and **"reset" expectations** are premature.

### 💰 **India’s Fuel Price Hikes (₹7.5/L in 11 Days)**
- **4 fuel hikes in 11 days** (May 15–25): ₹3.0 + ₹0.9 + ₹0.9 + ₹2.71 = **₹7.50/L increase**.
- **Purpose**: Offset **₹1,000 crore/day losses** for OMCs (IOCL, BPCL, HPCL) due to high crude, weak rupee, and stagnant retail rates.
- **Impact**:
  - Daily losses reduced by **~₹113 crore** post-latest hike → **44% recovery** of Q1 shortfall.
  - Each ₹0.5/L hike cuts losses by **7–11%**.
- **Positive signal**: Falling crude prices (Brent < $100) **improve OMCs’ financial outlook**, potentially easing future hikes.

## 🛢️ **Energy & Commodity Shifts**

### ☢️ **Solar Export Collapse in U.S.**
- **Anti-dumping & countervailing duties >230%** have **wiped out $2 billion Indian solar module market**.
- **Top manufacturers (e.g., Waaree Energies)** reported **near-zero exports in Q4 FY26**.
- **Strategic Shifts**:
  - Focus on **domestic demand**
  - Sourcing **cells from Ethiopia** to bypass U.S. origin rules
  - Exploring **EU, Middle East, Australia** markets (but face **Chinese competition**)
  - **U.S. production plans reconsidered** due to regulatory hurdles and talent shortages

### 🔋 **Indian Lithium-Ion Cell Industry: Race to Compete**
- **Cost gap**: 20–30% vs. Chinese rivals
- **Imports surged 56%** to **$4.6 billion in FY26** (2/3 from China)
- **Key Players**: Exide, Amara Raja, Agratas, Ola Electric – scaling production
- **Challenges**:
  - Lack of **local chemistry expertise** (NMC, LFP)
  - Limited **PLI fund disbursements**
  - Dependence on **Chinese equipment**
- **Industry Demands**:
  - Incentives on **raw materials**
  - **Battery Component & Materials Mission**
  - **Workforce upskilling**

## 💼 **Tech, AI & Digital Economy**

### 🤖 **AI Cost Crisis: Companies Scaling Back**
- **Microsoft** halted most **Claude Code** licenses; shifted to **GitHub Copilot CLI**
- **Uber burned entire 2026 AI budget in 4 months**, despite incentives
- **Root Cause**: **Token-based pricing** makes AI deployment **costlier than hiring humans**
  - Compute costs now **exceed employee wages**
  - Agentic AI increases token consumption exponentially
- **Executive View**: **Nvidia’s VP** acknowledges the **economic unsustainability** of current AI visions without **new cost models**

### 📱 **Flipkart’s Shopsy Overhaul: Gamification as Onboarding**
- Revamping **Shopsy** into a **feed-led, game-and-reward** app using **AI personalization**
- **Goal**: Build **digital comfort** in tier-2/3/4 users **before driving sales**
- **Model**: **No-commission** across 1,300 categories
- **Competitors**: Meesho, Amazon Bazaar, offline retail
- **Criticism**: Gamification ≠ conversion, but Flipkart sees it as **long-term user acquisition**

### 🧠 **EU Presses Banks on AI Security After Claude Mythos Leak**
- **Anthropic’s Claude Mythos** exposed **thousands of critical flaws** in OS/browsers
- **ECB Executive Board (Frank Elderson)** urges:
  - Faster **patch deployment**
  - **Collaboration with U.S. banks** (which have access to AI tools)
- **EU Actions**:
  - Negotiating with Anthropic for **AI-powered flaw testing**
  - **Mistral AI** exploring solutions with European banks
- **Key Message**: **AI can exploit vulnerabilities faster than patches** → **urgent security upgrade needed**
