  - Plug skill gaps
  - Counter pricing pressure
  - Accelerate AI transformation

  - Hexaware: $120M for SMC Squared
  - Firstsource: $28M for Pastdue Credit Solutions
  - Sasken: $40M for Borqs software unit

> 🔍 *Implication*: Indian IT is shifting from cost-driven services to **AI-powered innovation**, with M&A as the primary growth lever.

> 🔍 *Implication*: Even Tata’s digital ecosystem is under scrutiny. The **super-app strategy is being questioned**, and capital discipline is rising.

## 🏥 **Healthcare & Pharma: India’s Innovation Breakthrough**

### **1. Wockhardt – First Fully Indian-Origin New Drug Approved in US**
- **Zaynich (cefepime-zidebactam combo)** gets **FDA approval**.
- **Second Indian-origin NCE**, first **without foreign partner**.
- **Phase III results**: 89% efficacy vs. 68.4% for meropenem.
- **Launch plan**:
  - U.S.: 6–8 months
  - India, Europe, UK: parallel rollouts
  - Peak global sales: **$1.5B**
- **Commercialization**: Dedicated medical teams, hospital outreach, modest marketing (ICU-focused).
- **Pipeline**: 6 antibiotics, all with FDA **QIDP & fast-track** status.

> 🔍 *Implication*: India is breaking through in **innovation-driven pharma** — a major milestone for domestic R&D and global competitiveness.
