### 🏭 **Industrial & Manufacturing Growth**

- **Tata Motors**: Secured orders for **3,400+ electric commercial vehicles** (eCVs), including small trucks, large trucks, and buses across e-commerce, logistics, FMCG, mining, and transport sectors—signaling a shift from pilot projects to large-scale EV deployment.
- **Dalmia Bharat Cement**: Plans to raise **₹4,000 crore** via equity, GDRs/ADRs, convertible bonds to expand capacity from **49.5 MTPA to 110–130 MTPA by FY31**, supported by ₹6,800 crore Phase II investments and acquisition of Jaiprakash Associates’ assets (₹2,850 crore). Record FY26 EBITDA: ₹3,083 crore.
- **Voltas**: Achieved a **record 1 million room air-conditioners sold in first 3 months of FY26–27**, driven by product refresh, segmentation (premium/mid/value), strong distribution, and AI-enabled models. Rivals like LG also hit 1M in Q1, reflecting rebound from weak 2025 due to unseasonal rains.

### 🚗 **Automotive & Mobility**

- **India-UK CETA (Effective July 15)**:
  - Indian passenger vehicle makers (Maruti, Mahindra, Tata) gain **duty-free access** to UK EV, hybrid, and hydrogen car markets from **Year 6**.
  - Quotas: 6,800 units (under £20k, £20–40k), 4,000 (above £40k) in Year 6; rising to **88,000 units** (34k+34k+20k) by Year 15.
  - Aims to **double bilateral trade to $100B by 2030**, supporting “Make in India for the World”.
- **Overdrive Reviews**:
  - **MRF Steel Brace SP-01**: 0° steel-radial tire excels in high-speed stability, cornering, heat management (tested at 60°C). Outperforms stock Bridgestone; priced at ₹34,000/pair. Currently only available as replacement (CKD rules).
  - **Ducati Streetfighter V4 S**: Aggressive design, comfortable upright ergonomics, strong power delivery, agile handling—ideal for affluent urban riders despite firm suspension and limited pillion space.
  - **AMG-tuned GLC 53**: 449 PS, 640 Nm (with hybrid system), 0–100 km/h in **4.2 seconds**. Features AMG styling, rear-axle steering, race/drift modes, and family-friendly comfort—positioned as a **luxurious yet practical performance SUV**.

### 🛠️ **Logistics & Tech Innovation**

- **Delhivery Maps**: Launched **AI-native mapping platform** using data from **2B+ shipments** and **1B daily GPS pings**. Offers vehicle-aware routing, address validation, and accurate ETAs—differentiating from Google Maps and local players. Targeting e-commerce, quick-commerce, and gig-economy firms expanding into Tier 2–4 cities.
- **ONGC**: Natural gas now **exceeds crude oil** in production mix, set to drive **7–8% annual growth**. Supported by new field developments (DUDP, DSF, offshore 98/2), technical tie-up with BP, overseas assets (Russia, Mozambique, Venezuela), and reserve replacement >1.1x.

### 🏨 **Hospitality & Real Estate**

- **IHG Hotels & Resorts**: Aims for **400 properties in India by 2030**, shifting focus from Holiday Inn to **upscale/luxury brands** (InterContinental, Crowne Plaza, Kimpton, lifestyle). Expanding via Garner brand conversions and franchises, targeting metros and Tier 2 cities. Partnering with **Adani Airport Holdings** for airport-linked projects. Occupancy ~70%, strong domestic demand expected to drive **double-digit growth**.

### 🌿 **Energy & Sustainability**

- **ONGC**: Gas is becoming the **dominant segment** of its portfolio. Market-linked pricing reforms and rising domestic demand are key drivers. Offshore projects worth ₹33,000 crore underway.
- **Cement Sector**: Dalmia Bharat’s greenfield/brownfield expansions and acquisitions align with **6–7% CAGR in cement demand**. Focus on premium products (roof, column, foundation cements) for differentiation.

### 🌍 **Trade & Policy**

- **India-UK CETA** marks a strategic trade milestone, enhancing export competitiveness for Indian EVs and hybrids in the UK market—especially relevant for **Maruti’s eVITARA** and other "Make in India" exports.

### 🧘 **Public & Cultural Initiatives**

- **Prime Minister Modi** inaugurated **National Yoga Day 2026** in Kolkata, emphasizing yoga’s role in combating lifestyle diseases, improving flexibility, energy, and resilience across all age groups. Promoted as a tool for **national well-being and global soft power**.

### 📊 **Market Trends & Outlook**

- **RAC Market**: Recovering from 2025 slump due to unseasonal rains, boosted by **2024 heatwaves**. Annual demand: **12.5–14 million units**. Facing cost pressures from **new energy labeling norms, raw material inflation, rupee depreciation, and global inflation**.
- **EV Adoption**: Strong momentum in **commercial EVs** (Tata Motors), supported by infrastructure and policy push. E-commerce and quick-commerce growth fuel demand for better logistics tech (e.g., Delhivery Maps).

### 🔍 **Key Takeaways**

1. **India’s manufacturing exports are gaining global traction**, especially in EVs via CETA.
2. **AI and data-driven logistics innovation** (Delhivery Maps) is becoming a competitive moat.
3. **Energy transition is accelerating**, with gas overtaking oil in key players like ONGC.
4. **Luxury and performance vehicles** continue to attract affluent buyers, with hybrid and AMG tech enhancing appeal.
5. **Sustainability, digitalization, and export readiness** are central to India’s industrial strategy.
