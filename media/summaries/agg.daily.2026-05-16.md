### 🚀 **Tech & Innovation**

- **SpaceX** is set to go public on **Nasdaq by June 12, 2026**, under ticker **SPCX**, aiming for a **$75B raise at a $1.75T valuation** — the **largest IPO ever**.
  - Driven by Starlink’s rapid growth, swift SEC review, and Nasdaq’s “fast entry” rules.
  - Roadshow begins June 4; prospectus filed next week.
  - Lead underwriters: Morgan Stanley, Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan, BofA, Citi.
- **OpenAI** restructured leadership:
  - **Greg Brockman** gains expanded control over product & AI infrastructure.
  - New “super app” integrating **Codex, ChatGPT, Atlas** under Thibault Sottiaux.
  - **Nick Turley** leads enterprise products; **Ashley Alexander** (ex-Instagram) heads consumer.
  - **Sam Altman** remains on medical leave.
  - Focus: Revenue growth in coding & enterprise; trimming side projects ahead of IPO.
- **Dr. Reddy’s Laboratories** received **Health Canada approval** (April 28, 2026) for its generic **semaglutide injection** (2mg/4mg pens), the **first G7 approval** for this GLP-1 therapy.
  - Launch follows Indian rollout under brand **Obeda**; pricing competitive, not disclosed.

### 📈 **Investing & Markets**

- **Berkshire Hathaway** (led by CEO **Greg Abel**) executed a major portfolio shift:
  - **Tripled stake in Alphabet** (~58M shares, ~$17B).
  - Bought **$2.65B in Delta Air Lines** (6.1% stake), **$55M in Macy’s**.
  - Sold major holdings: **Amazon, Visa, Mastercard, UnitedHealth, Domino’s, Chevron (35%)**.
  - Net stock purchases: **$15.94B**, sales: **$24.09B** in Q1.
  - Shift reflects **tech & airline exposure**, signaling a departure from Buffett’s traditional sector focus.
- **Private Equity & Venture Capital Trends (India)**:
  - **Bain Capital, KKR, and others** are increasing exposure to India’s **consumer tech, AI-native, and B2B SaaS** sectors.
  - **Domestic investors now make up over 50%** of active funds, supported by rising global LPs.
  - Exit strategies diversifying: **buybacks, secondary sales, continuation vehicles** complement IPOs (still ~40% of exits).
  - Focus: **operational value creation**, supply chain resilience, and AI integration.

### 🏭 **Consumer & Manufacturing**

- **Allied Blenders & Distillers (ABD)**:
  - Q4 FY26: **9.1% op income growth**, **21.2% EBITDA rise**, but **PAT down 52%** due to **one-off tax and interest**.
  - No operational weakness; margins expected to normalize post-conflict (West Asia).
  - **Kion Blenders acquisition** secures raw materials & bottling in Andhra Pradesh → **doubles sales**, improves margins.
  - **ICONiQ** drives volume growth (12–13M cases MRR); **re-entry into Tamil Nadu** planned.
  - **International presence**: 23 → 36 countries; **15%+ YoY revenue growth**.
  - **FY27 Outlook**: Double-digit volume, mid-double-digit value growth, stable margins; **FY28 margin target: 18%** (after 200-bp boost from India-UK FTA).
- **Rosia Foods** (premium A2 ghee, raw honey, Vedic nutrition):
  - Currently **profitable (5–6% margin)**, **₹100Cr ARR**.
  - Competitive edge: **farm-to-table sourcing**, 5,000+ farmer partnerships.
  - New investment from **Aman Gupta’s Sales Through Ventures** → D2C & e-commerce expertise.
  - Goals: **₹150–200Cr ARR by FY27**, expand into **quick-commerce**, launch new Vedic products.

### 📡 **Telecom & Regulation**

- **Reliance Jio** removed **device-specific tariffs** following **TRAI directive**:
  - ₹123/28-day 4G plan (0.5GB/day, unlimited calls, 300 SMS) now available on **Nokia, Lava, itel**, and other 4G feature phones.
  - Previously restricted to **JioPhone/JioBharat devices** — a strategic move to **broaden base** among India’s ~220M feature phone users.
  - Aligns offline & online tariffs as mandated.
  - **Trade-off**: Loss of handset lock-in advantage, but potential **subscriber surge**.

### 🛰️ **Energy & Commodities**

- **Sherritt International** (Canadian miner):
  - Plans to **dissolve 50-50 JV with Cuba** at Moa nickel mine and **exit Energas (Cuba energy business)**.
  - Caused by **U.S. sanctions** on non-U.S. entities operating in Cuba.
  - Proposes **swap**: mining stake for full ownership of Alberta refinery + **C$277M equalization payment**.
  - Facing turmoil: board resignations, CFO departure, share price drop, delayed reporting.
  - Seeking **court order to expedite breakup**.
