### 📈 **Market & Economic Outlook**
- **Sensex & Nifty** dipped ~1% this week amid global uncertainty, but **mid-caps and Nifty Bank** showed resilience.
- **CIO Ravi Dharmishi** highlights a "stealth bull market" in **industrials and manufacturing**, driven by a **multi-year global capex super-cycle** focused on:
  - Supply chain resilience
  - Energy security
  - Strategic autonomy
- **Key beneficiaries**: High-end transformers, data-center infrastructure, renewables (solar, wind, hydrogen, batteries), and defense.
- **Valuations** appear high, but the **length and scale of capex justify long-term exposure**.
- **Policy tailwinds**: ALMM, PLI, and upcoming ALMM2 are accelerating India’s domestic renewable push to reduce reliance on China and build integrated supply chains.

---

### 🚀 **AI & Tech: The Next Wave of Capital Expenditure**
- **Global AI capex surge** is expanding beyond chips (TSMC, Samsung, SK Hynix) to **AI infrastructure**:
  - Advanced packaging
  - Server makers
  - Optical connectivity
  - Testing & power management
  - **Energy & renewable providers** (critical for data-center growth)
- **Estimated $70B+ new AI-related spending** on top of $750B already committed globally.
- **India’s position**: Lags in foundational AI layers (chips, accelerators, foundational models), but strong potential as:
  - A **major AI consumer**
  - A **global data-center hub** (due to cheap labor + abundant renewable power)
- **Risks**: 
  - US AI capex slowdown
  - Rising token costs
  - Job displacement in Indian IT services
- **Foreign capital flight**: $24B withdrawn from Indian equities in 2026 — shifting to AI-heavy markets like **Taiwan and Korea**, where semiconductor firms now hold over $1T in foreign holdings.

### ⚙️ **Corporate & Sector Updates**
#### **Renewables & Energy**
- **Adani Group**: Expanding AI-driven infrastructure; benefiting from global AI capex surge.
- **NLC India & Reliance**: Partnering to assess **underground lignite gasification** in Gujarat; aiming to create domestic **synthesis gas** and reduce LNG imports.
- **NLC’s ₹4,394Cr lignite-to-methanol plant** in Tamil Nadu aligns with India’s **₹37,500Cr coal/lignite gasification scheme** for energy security and net-zero goals.
- **SAIL**: Landed cost of limestone expected to rise from $23–24 to $35 due to West Asia crisis, but **steel pricing impact minimal** ($100–200). Mitigating via alternative shipping and Middle East sourcing.

#### **FMCG & Consumer**
- **Patanjali Foods**: Q4 net profit up **46% YoY** to ₹524Cr; full-year profit ₹1,814Cr on revenue ₹40.35B. Driven by strong rural demand, cooking oil sales, and FMCG growth (19.95% YoY).
- **Triveni Engineering & Industries**: Q4 net profit down **8%** (₹167.45Cr) due to higher sugarcane costs; PTB demerged into **Triveni Power Transmission Ltd** effective April 2026 (impact in FY27).

#### **Tech & Startups**
- **Blinkit**: Now **half of India’s quick-commerce market** and **profitable**. Outperforms Amazon due to **speed-first model**, not broad catalog. Focus: deepening SKUs (~80K in NCR) and customer loyalty.
- **Google Security Engineer Michele Spagnuolo**: Allegedly used internal search data to win **$1.2M on Polymarket**, charged with **fraud and money laundering**; ID traced via crypto withdrawal. Raises red flags on **data access controls** for senior tech staff.

#### **Infrastructure & Industrial**
- **SoftBank Group**: Plans **€75B ($87B)** investment in **AI data centers in France** (Le Bosquel & Dunkirk), with first site operational by 2028. Part of **France’s Choose France summit** push to become a **European AI hub**.
- **Reliance & NLC**: Joint feasibility study on **lignite gasification** — a strategic step toward energy self-reliance.

### 🔮 **Forward Outlook**
- **India’s path**: Not to become a chipmaker overnight, but to **leverage scale, renewables, and data** to become a **global AI infrastructure and services hub**.
- **Policy must focus** on:
  - **Targeted tax incentives** (e.g., for data centers)
  - **Strengthening data access controls** in tech firms
  - **Accelerating renewable integration** for AI infrastructure
- **Investors should**: Focus on **selective exposure** to **industrial, renewable, and defense sectors** — not headline indices.
