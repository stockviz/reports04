- **Titan** posted stellar Q4 results:

  - Coins now contribute **double-digit portfolio share**.

  - Strategic moves: Expanding mix to **14k, 18k, 9k gold**, launching **natural gemstone line**, and targeting **15–20% medium-term revenue growth** with focus on **North America, Singapore, Canada, and GCC**.

- **Toyota** announced a new **SUV factory in Bidkin, Maharashtra**, with capacity for **100,000 vehicles/year**, employing ~2,800 workers. Production starts in **H1 2029**—a strong vote of confidence in India’s manufacturing ecosystem and long-term auto ambitions.
  
- **Rolls-Royce** is targeting India as its **4th key market**, appointing former BP India head **Sashi Mukundan** to lead a **$10 billion push** across:
  - **Defence**: AMCA fighter engine bid (with local design center & tech transfer).
  - **Civil Aviation**: Expansion of MRO and engine services.
  - **Nuclear**: Exploring **Small Modular Reactors (SMRs)** under India’s upcoming **SHANTI nuclear bill**.
  - Strategy: Deep **localization**, industrial integration, and long-term partnerships.

- **Jawaharlal Nehru Port Authority (JNPA)** is preparing for an **IPO**, India’s largest container hub (handles **54% of national container traffic**).
  - FY25: Profit up **52%** to ₹2,053 crore; revenue ₹3,262 crore; assets ₹23,149 crore; reserves ₹14,613 crore.
  - Part of the **National Monetisation Pipeline**; similar to Kamarajar Port’s recent move.
  - Regulatory process underway (compliance, reporting); **valuation and pricing undefined**. Approval from ports ministry and authority required. Could attract strong institutional interest.

### **E. Tech & Digital Services**
- **Crown Worldwide Group (HK)** to **double Indian investment to $30 million** over 2 years:
  - $15M allocated to expand **information management & workplace services** driven by **GCC demand, hybrid work, and data privacy**.
  - Shift from relocation logistics to **enterprise infrastructure**: records management, digitization, asset & data governance.
  - New **4.5-acre Coimbatore facility** (468,000 SCE, expandable to 720,000).
  - AI-driven digitization, **20% electric fleet**, **40% renewable energy**.
  - Target: **₹500 crore+ Indian revenue by 2030** (from ₹350 crore in FY25), at **10–12% market CAGR**.
