### 💼 **Industry Trends & Shifts**
#### **Pharma**
- **Sun Pharma’s Organon deal** signals a **strategic pivot** from low-margin generics to high-value specialty medicines (biosimilars, women’s health).
- **Global trend**: Patent cliffs, pricing pressure, and regulatory shifts (e.g., FDA interchangeability rules) are pushing Indian pharma firms toward **innovation and diversification**.

#### **IT Services**
- **Infosys, HCLTech, Tech Mahindra** are **rejecting low-margin contracts** despite top-line trade-offs.  
  - HCLTech: 36% drop in deal wins after walking away from ~$1B in contracts.  
  - Infosys: Trimmed FY27 guidance after declining a European deal.  
  - **Drivers**: AI-driven automation compressing deal values; margin preservation (Infosys: 20.3%, HCLTech: 17.2%).  
  - **Analyst View**: Structural shift toward profitability, not temporary pricing issue – **mirroring TCS’s recent stance**.

#### **Consumer & FMCG**
- **Diet Coke shortage** creates a **summer opportunity** for zero-sugar D2C brands:  
  - **Paper Boat Zero, Chinni Kum, Zyro, Jimmy’s, Pepping** leveraging PET bottle advantage (aluminum scarcity).  
  - Rapid growth: Zero-sugar segment at **98% CAGR**, though still small (1.3% household penetration).  
  - **Challenges**: High GST (40%), rising input costs (20–25% for cans), and sustainability of advantage post-packaging normalization.

#### **Paints & Construction**
- **Berger Paints**: Revenue surged from ₹3,000cr to ₹12,000cr.  
  - Driven by premiumization, urbanization, repainting demand.  
  - **Margin Expansion**: Oil-light product mix, focus on high-margin segments (decorative, waterproofing, industrial).  
  - **Tech Edge**: One-Eye supply chain, AI-powered My Color app.  
  - **Sustainability**: Solar-powered plants, low-VOC/zero-VOC products, platinum-LEED building.

#### **Technology & AI**
- **Autodesk (Kamalika Gupta-Perez)**:  
  - Embedding **AI, cloud, and data** into design/operations for end-to-end integration.  
  - **Sustainability must be designed-in**.
