Gold Import Stalls Over GST Clarification and DGFT Licensing Issues

MMTC

Banks cannot import gold because the DGFT has not issued a post‑April GST exemption notification, leading to a 3 % GST charge that banks refuse to pay. Simultaneously, the DGFT has halted renewal and issuance of import licences for gold dories, allowing only one selective licence, and has shifted jewelry imports from the free to the restricted category. Consequently, gold imports, dory imports, and jewelry imports are effectively stalled, raising fears of market shortages ahead of Akshay Tritiya and the wedding season. The industry doubts any imminent gold monetisation scheme and speculates that the delays may be linked to the weakening rupee. Immediate clarification from the finance ministry or DGFT is needed to avert supply constraints
