Market Coupling Case: Key Electricity Appellate Tribunal (APTEL) Hearing Today | CNBC TV18

IEX

The Indian Energy Exchange (IEX) faces a market‑coupling challenge from the CERC’s 2025 order, which exchanges allege was tainted by insider trading and a SEBI‑reported scheme that yielded illegal gains of over ₹170 crore. The Electricity Appellate Tribunal (APTEL) is hearing the case at 10:30 am, noting the alleged misconduct and indicating it may act if foul play is proven. A CERC lawyer announced a request for a formal opinion on whether the coupling order should be withdrawn, prompting a 12‑13% rise in IEX shares. Further clarification is expected after the APTEL hearing.
