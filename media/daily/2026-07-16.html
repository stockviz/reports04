ACME Solar Secures ₹2,647 cr Funding for 450 MW FDRE Project

ACMESOLAR RECLTD SJVN

ACME Solar Holdings announced a ₹2,646.64 crore, 20‑year loan from REC Ltd to finance its 450 MW/1,800 MWh Assured Peak Power (FDRE) project, which pairs solar generation with a battery energy storage system to deliver four hours of peak power during non‑solar periods. The under‑construction pipeline features 3,580 MW of projects with signed PPAs
