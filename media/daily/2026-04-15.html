India’s Electric Cycle Market Accelerates as Start‑ups and Legacy Brands Compete for Growth

OLAELEC HEROMOTOCO

India’s e‑cycle sector is entering a rapid growth phase, with revenues projected to reach $720 million in FY26 and over $850 million by FY27. Policy support—including Delhi’s draft EV policy restricting ICE two‑wheelers in delivery fleets, exemption from registration/licensing for e‑cycles under 250 W, and a lower 5 % GST—boosts demand. Experts anticipate FY27 as a tipping point, where execution, scale, affordability and real‑world utility will determine market winners
