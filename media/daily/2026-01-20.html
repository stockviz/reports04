  • Demand outlook: In the very short term demand may dip due to higher prices and extra stock built in Q3, but it should stabilize once inventories normalize (by Jan).   • Pricing strategy: Price hikes of 5‑10% have already begun (driven by BEE norm changes and raw‑material spikes). Full impact will be seen when seasonal demand peaks (Feb–Mar). Have Announced ₹2,400 Cr Of Capex, Announce ₹2,000 Cr Capex Going Forward: Himadri Speciality

HSCL

Himadri Specialty Chemicals’ CMD Anurag Chaudhry outlined a five‑year growth plan driven by ≈₹2,400 crore of already‑announced capex and a further ₹2,000 crore to be disclosed later. Key projects include:

  • Doubling specialty carbon‑black capacity to 120 kt (commercial production from Q3 FY26) – the world’s largest single site.
  • New high‑value products (anthracene & carbazole) – ₹120 crore.

Overall, Himadri’s capex roadmap aims to boost top‑line revenue, sustain high returns, expand export exposure, and significantly lift profitability by FY 28.
