Nalco’s FY27 Outlook: Domestic Alumina Push, Capacity Ramp‑Up, and Cost Pressures

Chairman Brijendra Pratap Singh discussed weak alumina pricing (forecast $3,000‑$3,100/ton) amid excess supply, expecting only modest recovery post‑geopolitical easing. The new 1 Mt alumina refinery will be commissioned by June, aiming for 50‑60% capacity (≈200‑300 kt) this year, up from an earlier target of 500 kt. Export share remains ~50‑55% of 2.1 Mt production, with ~40‑45% of exports going to the Middle East; domestic sales are slated to rise from 138 kt to 250 kt.
