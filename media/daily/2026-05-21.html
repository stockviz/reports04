Chicago MLS Blocks Zillow Listings After Data Dispute

IBREALEST BCG

Midwest Real Estate Data (MRED) halted Zillow’s access to about 43,000 Chicago-area listings, citing violations of its licensing agreement after Zillow refused to show a handful of homes that were first marketed privately by Compass. Zillow, which sued MRED for allegedly colluding with Compass to hide listings, argues its policy of excluding listings advertised elsewhere for over 24 hours is lawful. The dispute escalates a broader conflict between Zillow and the megabrokerage over market transparency and competition

STANLEY

OpenAI plans to confidentially file for a U.S. IPO within weeks, targeting a September debut and a valuation up to $1 trillion. The move follows the resolution of Elon Musk’s legal challenge and aims to coincide with SpaceX’s anticipated filing, drawing investor attention to both companies.
