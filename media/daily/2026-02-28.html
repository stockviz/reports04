CCPA Fines Raising Superstars ₹8 Lakh for Misleading Infant Development Claims

ADANIENPP1

The Central Consumer Protection Authority imposed an ₹8 lakh penalty on edtech firm Raising Superstars Enterprises for advertising unverified, time‑bound developmental milestones (e.g., crawling at 3 months, walking at 8 months, 200‑word vocabulary by 18 months) linked to its Prodigy Framework Program.
