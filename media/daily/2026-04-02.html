All Banks Are Active In Onshore And Offshore Currency Market: DSP Finance tells

AYE

Title: RBI’s New Net‑Open‑Position Cap Spurs Currency Arbitrage Concerns, Says DSP Finance CEO

Summary:
DSP Finance CEO Jaish Mehta explains that the RBI’s recent circular limiting net open positions (NOP) to $100 million daily from April 10 aims to curb arbitrage between onshore and offshore rupee markets.
