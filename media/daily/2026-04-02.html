Aluminum Soars Amid Geopolitical Disruptions

GLOBAL STANLEY

Crude oil and aluminum are the only commodities rising, with LME aluminum up 3% and cash premiums exceeding three‑month contracts. RBI’s Crackdown on On‑shore/Off‑shore Currency Arbitrage

KOTAKBANK

The RBI issued fresh circulars to delink the on‑shore and off‑shore INR markets, prohibiting banks from using NDFs for hedging or arbitrage and ordering them to liquidate excess dollar positions by April 10. This aims to curb speculation and arbitrage that were transmitting offshore premiums (driven by oil shocks) to the on‑shore market, close loopholes that allowed banks to shift exposure to corporates, and shift price‑setting power to the RBI. The forced unwind caused a sharp sell‑off in the dollar‑rupee pair, while the longer‑term impact on equities and capital flows will depend on RBI’s future intervention stance
