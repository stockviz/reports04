Pricing Power: The Key to Sustainable Profit Growth

PAGEIND TITAN MCX

Pricing power is a company’s ability to raise prices without losing demand, driven by brand trust, switching costs, limited alternatives, or low share of customer spending. It boosts revenue and margins, especially gross margins, by allowing price hikes that outpace cost increases. This leads to stronger cash generation, cleaner balance sheets, higher ROCE, and premium market valuations. Indicators include stable gross margins, resilience during inflation, premium pricing versus peers, high market share, and low churn. Unlike volume‑driven growth, pricing power requires less capital and offers long‑term wealth creation for exceptional firms
