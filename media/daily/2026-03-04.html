Goldman Sachs’ head of private credit explained that recent “gating” of its funds – limiting investor withdrawals – is a deliberate tool, not a flaw. He said the practice protects the portfolio’s liquidity, ensures consistent performance, and shields remaining investors from forced sales during market stress.
