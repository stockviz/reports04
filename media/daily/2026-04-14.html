Multi‑Asset Funds Outshine Traditional Hybrids: Performance, Appeal, and Portfolio Role

ICICIBANK 5PAISA

In a detailed discussion on NDTV Profit’s “Your Money Matters,” host Alex Mathew and co‑founder of Wealthy.in, Aditya Garwal, examined the surge in inflows to multi‑asset funds, which now dominate the hybrid category in FY2026. They highlighted that multi‑asset funds delivered the best returns over the past one‑ and three‑year periods, boasting a lower downside capture ratio (≈27%) and reduced volatility (standard deviation 9‑11%) compared to equity benchmarks like the NIFTY 50. The conversation covered why investors are gravitating toward asset‑allocation products—seeking diversified exposure to equities, fixed income, commodities, real estate, and global markets within a single fund—especially as many investors now hold all assets through financial products rather than direct holdings. Ultimately, they concluded that multi‑asset funds are becoming a core, low‑volatility component of portfolios, offering diversified risk‑adjusted returns and simplifying asset allocation for investors
