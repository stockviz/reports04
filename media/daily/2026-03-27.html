Infosys led 2025 with an ₹18,000 cr buyback, while TCS, Infosys and Wipro have collectively returned over ₹185,000 cr via buybacks in the past decade. A tax department clarification notes the 12.5% rate applies only to promoter‑level buybacks, so individual shareholders will still face long‑term capital‑gain tax (≈20‑30%), making the lower rate potentially beneficial for them
