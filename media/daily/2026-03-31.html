ECB doubts banking experience of Monte dei Paschi CEO candidate

MONTECARLO BLSE DHANI

The European Central Bank (ECB) is uneasy about the banking credentials of Fabrizio Palermo, the proposed CEO of Monte dei Paschi di Siena (MPS). Palermo, formerly head of state lender Cassa Depositi e Prestiti (CDP), lacks direct banking operational experience, and the ECB does not consider CDP equivalent to a bank. Palermo will only undergo ECB vetting if shareholders select him over current CEO Luigi Lovaglio on April 15. The ECB’s assessment will also factor in governance weaknesses that affect the bank’s capital requirements
