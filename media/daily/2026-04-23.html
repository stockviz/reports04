Weatherford International expects its earnings to be further pressured by the Iran war, projecting a $30‑$50 million loss in the first half of the year as oilfield activity in Iraq, Qatar and Kuwait is paused and freight costs rise. Thoma Bravo Nears Deal to Hand Medallia to Creditors

DELTACORP 5PAISA APOLLOPIPE

Private equity firm Thoma Bravo is close to an agreement to transfer customer‑experience software company Medallia to its lenders, wiping out roughly $5.1 billion in equity for Thoma Bravo and co‑investors. Medallia, acquired for $6.4 billion in 2021, is weighed down by about $3 billion of debt owed to Blackstone, KKR, Apollo and Antares Capital. Falling valuations, competition from AI‑driven solutions, and high leverage have pressured the firm, leading lenders to consider taking control, with debt holders marking the obligations at discounted values while some loans remain at par
