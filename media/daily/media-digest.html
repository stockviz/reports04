Infosys Q3: Adjusted EBIT Growth Guidance At 21.2%; Raises FY26 Constant Currency Revenue Guidance

INFY

Infosys posted Q3 results that beat expectations, with its stop‑line earnings rising 0.6% versus a flat consensus. Adjusted EBIT margin came in at 21.2% (reported margin fell to 18.4% after a one‑time ₹1,289 cr labor‑court charge, dragging net profit down 9.4%).

Key highlights:
- FY‑26 constant‑currency revenue growth guidance lifted from 2‑3% to 3‑3.5%.
- Adjusted EBIT growth guidance set at 21.2%.
- Deal wins surged to $4.8 bn, buoyed by the NHS contract.
- Headcount added ~11,000 employees over the last two quarters, signaling strong demand.

Management stressed robust execution, strong pipeline in financial services, energy, utilities and resources, and positioning Infosys as the AI partner of choice for its largest clients.
