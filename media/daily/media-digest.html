KG‑D6 Gas Production Decline and Mitigation Plans

RELIANCE

Natural gas output from Reliance‑BP’s KG‑D6 block fell about 7% YoY to 24.8 mmscmd in Q1 FY27, continuing a natural decline that began mid‑2025. Production peaked at 30.6 mmscmd in Q1 2024 and has dropped 19% since. Reliance (66.7% stake) and BP (33.3%) invested ~$5 bn in the R‑Cluster, Satellite, and MJ projects, which were expected to supply ~15% of India’s gas demand. Gas price fell to $8.89/MMBtu, while condensate price rose to $107.4/bbl. The companies acknowledge the decline, claim it is less severe than expected, and say a mitigation plan is forthcoming, though details and timelines remain undisclosed
