AAI’s Profit Hinges on Privatized Airports as Core Network Bleeds

SAIL GMRAIRPORT

The Airports Authority of India (AAI) posted a FY25 net profit of ₹7,233 crore, but over 75% of its 122 airports incurred losses totaling ₹1,600 crore. More than 30% of the profit came from under‑recovery payments of ₹2,232 crore from six PPP airports (Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Lucknow, Mangaluru, Guwahati, Thiruvananthapuram), a one‑time boost that is unlikely to recur. The case, stemming from alleged misrepresentations about the aircraft’s flight‑control software that contributed to the 2018 Lion Air and 2019 Ethiopian Airlines crashes that killed 346 people, will remain dismissed
