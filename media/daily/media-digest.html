Indian firms such as Cipla, Zydus Lifesciences, Alkem Laboratories and GLS Pharma are being queried for manufacturing capabilities, FDA registration status, and market approvals. The FDA is willing to consider products from non‑FDA‑registered facilities with a strong compliance history. The shortage is expected to persist through 2026, and India’s large, FDA‑registered pharmaceutical sector, which accounts for a substantial share of US anti‑cancer drug imports, is positioned to help address the gap
