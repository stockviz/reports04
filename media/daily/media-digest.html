Young Woman Testifies About Childhood Social‑Media Addiction in Landmark Trial

FDA Introduces Speed‑Based Bonus Pilot for Drug Reviewers

FDA Commissioner Marty Makary announced a pilot program to award quarterly bonuses to drug reviewers who finish assessments ahead of schedule, using weighted time‑savings, quality ratings, and task complexity as metrics. The plan, targeting only staff directly involved in drug reviews, aims to boost efficiency without compromising safety, but raises concerns about fairness across teams, potential pressure to rush evaluations, and public perception of the agency’s independence, especially amid staff shortages and criticism of recent regulatory decisions
