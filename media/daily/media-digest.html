IIT Kanpur Study Finds No Evidence E20 Damages Engines, Challenges Manufacturer Claims

HONDAPOWER TV18BRDCST

Researchers at IIT Kanpur reported that blending ethanol up to 20% (E20) does not significantly affect fuel efficiency or cause engine damage in both new and old vehicles, contradicting some automakers’ cautions about ethanol limits.
