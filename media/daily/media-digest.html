5PAISA

  • Return‑type timelines – Original return = staggered due dates.
    • Belated return = up to 31 Dec (unchanged).
    • Revised return = up to 31 Mar (extended from Dec). Must first file original or belated return.
    • Updated return (ITRU) = up to 24 months after assessment year (now 29 Mar 2029 for FY 25‑26) with 25‑50 % penalty; can be filed even if assessment has started but not completed.

  • TCS rate cuts – Foreign tour packages & travel 5 %→ 2 %; Liberalised Remittance Scheme for education/medical 5 %→ 2 %; unchanged 20 % rate for overseas investment purchases over ₹10 L.

  • NRI property transactions – TAN requirement removed; buyers can file Form 26QB directly.
    Automatic, faster lower‑TDS certificates to be introduced for NRI seller transactions (previously 60‑90 days).

  • Foreign income/assets disclosure – Six‑month voluntary window announced; filing within it attracts minimal penalty and avoids prosecution.

  • Upcoming tax‑bill changes – “Assessment Year” terminology to be dropped; only “Financial Year” will be used, simplifying language but not altering substance.

  • Securities Transaction Tax (STT) – Government emphasises “investor” vs. “trader” classification; STT to be applied to curb speculative F&O trading, treating F&O income as business income in ITR.

Budget 2026 | India Takes A Big Step To Cut Carbon Emissions | N18V | CNBC TV18

BPCL RELIANCE ONGC

The 2026 Indian budget allocates ₹20,000 crore for carbon capture, utilization, and storage (CCUS) to curb emissions from steel, refineries, and chemicals without shutting down industry. CCUS involves three steps: capturing CO₂ from plant exhaust, reusing it for fuels, chemicals or building materials, and storing the remainder deep underground. India is still in the pilot phase, with companies like Reliance, ONGC (pilot at Gandhar oil field), IOCL, HPCL, and BPCL planning larger projects. Government funding aims to scale up the technology, helping Indian exporters avoid EU carbon‑border taxes. Execution, not just funding, will determine success.
