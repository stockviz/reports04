CCI Dismisses Abuse‑of‑Dominance Complaint Against BookMyShow

IMAGICAA PAYTM

The Competition Commission of India rejected a complaint alleging that BookMyShow’s parent, Big Tree Entertainment, abused its dominant position in online movie‑ticket booking by imposing exclusive deals with cinemas. While CCI confirmed BookMyShow’s dominant market standing, it found insufficient evidence that such arrangements denied market access or foreclosed competition, noting the continued operation of rivals like Paytm, Justickets, and Amazon. Consequently, no violation of Sections 4(2)(a)(i), 4(2)(b)(i) or 4(2)(c) was established, and the case was closed
