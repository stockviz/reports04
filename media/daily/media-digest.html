Dutch regulator RDW approved FSD after its own testing, but did not comment on the disputed figures. Swedish and other European authorities are reviewing the data, with concerns that Tesla’s marketing may not reflect independent, verifiable safety evidence. Approval of FSD is crucial for Tesla’s European sales recovery, but widespread adoption requires convincing evidence beyond the company’s contested statistics
