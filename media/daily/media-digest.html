Oil Market Outlook Amid Escalating West Asia Conflict

Analysts debate de‑escalation versus escalation of the West Asia war, with Art Berman outlining a base case of $100‑$150 Brent, a best‑case fallback to $80 if hostilities cease, and a permanent risk premium of at least $10 per barrel.
