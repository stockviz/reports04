Aluminum Prices Surge to Four‑Year High on West Asia Conflict and Supply Tightening

MAANALU ATHERENERG

Aluminum has outperformed other base metals, climbing about 5% in the past week, 10% over the month and 13% YTD, reaching a four‑year peak.
