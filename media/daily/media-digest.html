“Government Cannot Retain Invalid Tax”: SC Orders ₹500 Cr Refund to Adani Power’s Mundra Plant

ADANIPOWER

The Supreme Court ruled that Adari Power’s electricity sales from its Mundra SEZ plant are exempt from customs duty, declaring the levy “invalid tax.” The court overturned a 2019 Gujarat High Court decision and reaffirmed its 2015 stance that the duty lacked legal authority. It ordered the customs department to refund the roughly ₹500 crore collected, directing the payment be made within eight weeks. This decision marks a major win for Adani Power.
