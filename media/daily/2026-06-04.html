SEBI Issues Interim Order Against Rajesh Exports and Promoter Over Financial Misrepresentation

RAJESHEXPO

SEBI has issued a 109‑page interim order after a four‑year investigation (April 2020‑March 2024) into Rajesh Exports Ltd and its promoter Rajesh Mehta. The regulator identified material violations in four key areas: (1) systemic failure to substantiate financial figures; (2) non‑availability of financials for subsidiaries that contribute over 95% of consolidated revenue, suggesting misreporting; (3) selective and incomplete disclosures; and (4) elimination of intra‑group transactions to conceal entity‑specific risk, cash‑flows, and related‑party exposure. The order restrains Rajesh Mehta from buying, selling, or dealing in Rajesh Exports securities and mandates the company to provide true‑and‑fair disclosures of its financial statements and related‑party transactions
