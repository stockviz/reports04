India’s Beer Sector Fuels Near Double‑Digit Top‑Line Growth Amid Cost Pressures

UBL

United Breweries MD Vivek Gupta says the Indian beer market is enjoying strong sales, driven by state reforms, favorable weather and a shift among younger consumers toward beer, with volume growth in the high single‑digit range and modest price gains. However, profitability is squeezed by rising input costs—glass bottles, aluminium cans and imported materials—all inflated by the war‑related supply shock and a weak rupee. Gupta expects margin recovery as the industry works to mitigate these cost pressures, noting that balanced monsoon patterns matter more than short‑term heat spikes for sustained demand

BlackSoil NBFC Expands into Solar Financing with CreditFair Acquisition

ASHIKA BCG

BlackSoil’s NBFC arm has acquired Credit Fair’s solar financing business for roughly ₹45 crore, adding ₹160 crore of assets and targeting loan sizes of ₹3‑5 lakh for retail and larger tickets for bigger clients. The move deepens its B2B2C footprint, boosts its climate‑focused portfolio (now 17% of holdings), and aligns with its strategy of diversified, bottom‑up growth and sector consolidation. BlackSoil, which managed about ₹2,500 crore in assets and disbursed ₹12,212 crore across 444 companies by March 2026, aims to leverage its structured credit expertise to support India’s solar rooftop expansion while maintaining a comfortable liquidity position
